Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 October 2025

Who we are

Technical Investment Association (“TIA”, “we”, “us”) is a university club focused on investing and technology. This policy explains what personal data we process and why.

What data we collect

  • Membership sign-ups (Google Forms): name, email address and any other fields shown on the form.
  • Site analytics: basic, privacy-friendly analytics provided by Vercel. We do not use advertising cookies.

How we use your data

  • To administer memberships and communicate about events and club news.
  • To understand aggregate website usage and improve the site.
  • To manage social media presence where members have consented (see Terms & Conditions for media consent).

Where your data is stored

Membership form responses are stored in TIA’s shared Google Drive accessible only to current admins with appropriate access.

Legal basis

  • Consent: when you submit the membership form and, where relevant, for media/PR uses.
  • Legitimate interests: running the club and its website in a way members reasonably expect.

Your rights

You can request to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (note: this ends your membership)

Retention

We keep membership data for as long as you are a member. When a member resigns, related membership data is deleted.

Cookies & analytics

The site uses Vercel’s basic analytics. We do not set advertising cookies and do not track individual profiles for marketing.

Third-party links

Our website links to Instagram; we do not embed social media widgets. Linked sites are governed by their own policies.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact the TIA administration at technicalinvestmentassociation@gmail.com or via the details provided on our site.

This policy is for general information and may be updated from time to time.
